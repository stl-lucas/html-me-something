The Art of Brewing Beer

What is Brewing Beer

Brewing beer is the process of milling and steeping grain, boiling the liquid with hops, chilling, adding yeast, and waiting for fermentation to complete.

What do you need?

In order to brew a batch of beer you will need a few key pieces of equipment.

  • Brew Kettle (10g stainless steel)
  • Fermentation Vessel (6.5g plastic bucket with lid)
  • Fermentation Lock
  • Wort Chiller
  • Paddle or Stirring Spoon
  • Strainer
  • Ingredient Kit (Malt, Hops, Yeast & Water)
  • Timer & Notepad
  • 48 - 52 empty bottles

How do you brew?

  1. Heat 6 or 7 gallons of water to a boil. Before reaching a boil, add your malt extract and stir in thoroughly.
  2. When you reach a boil, add hops and start a timer for 60 minutes.
  3. After 60 minutes, chill the wort (liquid) to below 80 degrees.
  4. Pour into your fermentation bucket and add yeast.
  5. Seal your fermentator and place in 60 - 65 degree for about 2 weeks.
  6. Bottle the beer with priming sure and let sit for at least 1 week before drinking.
  7. Enjoy!
